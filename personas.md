# Persona-Based Synthesis Framework

When generating a briefing, adjust the "Executive Gotcha" and "Top 3 Items" based on who the leader is meeting.

## Persona A: The CFO (The Economic Buyer)
- **Primary Focus:** ROI, Cost-to-Value ratio, and contract consolidation.
- **Synthesis Priority:** Highlight "Underutilized Licenses" or "Calculated Cost Savings" since the last call.
- **Gotcha:** Any mentions of budget cuts in recent earnings calls or news.

## Persona B: The VP of Product (The Gatekeeper)
- **Primary Focus:** Feature parity, roadmap alignment, and friction-free workflows.
- **Synthesis Priority:** Highlight specific technical blockers or "Missing Feature" requests that have been escalated.
- **Gotcha:** A specific bug that impacted their team’s productivity this week.

## Persona C: The CSM/Account Manager (The Daily User)
- **Primary Focus:** Ease of use and support responsiveness.
- **Synthesis Priority:** Highlight the "Support Ticket Volume" trend and sentiment of the last 3 tickets.
- **Gotcha:** If they have a high "NPS" score but low "Product Adoption" (The False Positive).
