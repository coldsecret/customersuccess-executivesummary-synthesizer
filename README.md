# 📋 Executive Summary Synthesizer

> **The AI "Chief of Staff" for Senior CS Leaders.**

In high-stakes SaaS environments, information is often scattered across Slack, CRM, Gainsight, and meeting recordings. This skill solves "Information Overload" by synthesizing these disparate sources into a high-density briefing.

---

## 🎯 The Problem
Senior leaders often spend 30-60 minutes prepping for a single executive call. This skill reduces that prep time to **30 seconds** by surfacing the "Signal" from the "Noise."

## ✨ Brilliant Features
* **Multi-Source Fusion:** Ingests data from 5+ sources simultaneously.
* **30-Second Briefing:** Generates a high-density summary designed for pre-call situational awareness.
* **The "Executive Gotcha":** Surfaces small, easily-missed details that could impact a high-level conversation.

---

## 🛠️ How to Use
1. **Upload** your meeting transcripts, Slack export, or Gainsight notes to Claude.
2. **Apply** this skill.
3. **Command:** *"Give me the situational briefing for this account before my 2:00 PM call."*
