# Forensic Signals: Passive Dissatisfaction (CS Leadership)

Use this guide to identify risks that aren't captured by health scores.

## Category 1: Sentiment Drift
- **The "Transactional" Shift:** Client stops asking about roadmap/strategy and only focuses on support ticket status.
- **Shortened Responses:** One-word answers in Slack or email from previously engaged stakeholders.
- **Tone Softening:** Moving from constructive criticism to polite indifference (this is often a sign they have already checked out).

## Category 2: Stakeholder Ghosting
- **Executive Substitution:** The VP no longer joins calls; they send a junior associate instead.
- **Meeting Fatigue:** Frequent rescheduling or "no-shows" for QBRs without proactive rebooking.
- **Champion Silence:** Your internal advocate stops sharing "insider" information about budget or org changes.

## Category 3: Value Gaps
- **Goal Amnesia:** Client asks "What are we paying for again?" during a renewal window.
- **Integration Stagnation:** The product is used, but new features or advanced use cases are being ignored.
- **Competitor Name-Dropping:** Passive mentions of features other platforms have during casual conversation.
